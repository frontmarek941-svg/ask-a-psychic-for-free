About Ask-a-Psychic

Free psychic Q&A — ask one question, get a real answer fast.

Our Story

Ask-a-Psychic was launched in 2024 to address a common frustration: people with a single burning question often had to book (and pay for) an entire reading session just to get one answer. Our founders — Elena V., Marcus D., and Aria S. — envisioned a simpler approach: submit your question, and a qualified psychic provides a thoughtful, personalised answer. No registration, no session bookings, no hidden fees. The Q&A format makes psychic guidance as accessible as asking a trusted friend for advice, except the friend happens to have genuine intuitive gifts.

Founded: 2024 | Founded by: Elena V., Marcus D., and Aria S.

Headquarters: Remote-first, United States

Our Mission

To provide accessible, honest, and compassionate psychic guidance to anyone who needs clarity — without financial barriers or hidden fees.

Our Values

  • Integrity — We provide honest, authentic readings without manipulation or false promises.
  • Accessibility — Spiritual guidance should be available to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances.
  • Compassion — Every interaction is treated with empathy, respect, and genuine care.
  • Transparency — We are open about our methods, qualifications, and what you can expect from a reading.
  • Privacy — Your personal information and reading details are kept strictly confidential.
